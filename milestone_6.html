Question 4

Service #1

  • What is the URL for the full GetCapabilities request to the service?

    • http://nsidc.org/cgi-bin/atlas_north?service=WMS&request=GetCapabilities&version=1.1.1

  • What is the Name of the service?

    • OGC:WMS

  • What Format(s) are available for GetMap requests from the service?

    • tiff, gif, png; mode=24bit, jpeg, vnd.wap.wbmp, & svg+xml

  • How many layers are included in the service (including nesting layers)?

    • 95

    Service #2

  • What is the URL for the full GetCapabilities request to the service?

    • http://wms.jpl.nasa.gov/wms.cgi?Service=WMS&Version=1.1.1&Request=GetCapabilities

  • What is the Name of the service?

    • OGC:WMS

  • What Format(s) are available for GetMap requests from the service?

    • jpeg, png, geotiff, tiff, vnd.google-earth.kml+xml

  • How many layers are included in the service (including nesting layers)?

    • 17
